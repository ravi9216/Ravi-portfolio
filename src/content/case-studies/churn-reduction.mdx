---
title: Reducing SaaS Churn
result: Reduced churn by 15%
---

## Problem

Enterprise customers were dropping during the first 30 days.

## Customer Insights

Interviews revealed onboarding confusion and missing success metrics.

## Product Strategy

Prioritized onboarding flows using RICE scoring.

## Tech Architecture

Built event-driven lifecycle triggers and experimentation pipelines.

## Roadmap & Prioritization

Focused on onboarding automation and engagement loops.

## Launch & Execution

Rolled out new lifecycle messaging and product education.

## Impact Metrics

• Churn ↓ 15%  
• Activation ↑ 22%

## Lessons Learned

Customer success signals must be embedded inside product flows.
